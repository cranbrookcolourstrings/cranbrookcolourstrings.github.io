Classes

Our Music Kindergarten Classes take place at Church House, which is located behind Vestry Hall in the centre of Cranbrook.

Classes take place on Monday mornings and Tuesday afternoons.

Contact us to find out which class would be appropriate for your child and to sign up for a three week trial at a reduced rate.

The sooner you start, the greater the benefits!
